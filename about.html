The oldest trekking community in North Kashmir!

33 Himalayan Hangout started out initially in the form of Bandipora Youth Federation, founded by H.E. Padma Shri Faisal Ali Dar.

To divert the attention of the youth from the then decades long insurgency and unrest, he founded the federation and Ali Sports Academy which trains students in 18 sports, coaching around 17,000 children for a fee of ₹50 per year. His academy has produced countless gold medalists including Tajamul Islam and Abida, both bagging gold medal for India.
